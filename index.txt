# about

About me

 Enthusiastic and highly motivated junior software engineer
            well trained in designing, developing, and implementing
            applications and solutions using a wide range of technologies
            and programming languages. Deep rooted passion in web
            development and design and also in artificial intelligence. My
            goal is to become a valuable mutual asset by providing
            software engineering expertise that will contribute to the
            company's purpose and profitability.

My job is to build your website so that it is functional and user-friendly but at the same time attractive. Moreover, I add personal touch to your product and make sure that is eye-catching and easy to use. My aim is to bring across your message and identity in the most creative way. I created web design for many famous brand companies.
